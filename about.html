About Us

Welcome to our website

NexTag, a prominent online shopping and comparison website, emerged as a pioneering platform that revolutionized the way consumers research and make informed purchasing decisions. Founded in 1999, NexTag has provided users with a valuable tool for comparing products, prices, and retailers across a wide range of categories. This innovative platform aggregates data from various online sellers, allowing users to easily find the best deals and products, making it a go-to destination for savvy shoppers seeking convenience and cost savings. Over the years, NexTag has continually evolved to meet the changing needs of online consumers, maintaining its position as a trusted resource in the world of e-commerce.

Our Story

Nextag, initially founded as GoTo.com in 1999, transitioned into a prominent price comparison and online shopping website, allowing consumers to compare prices from various online retailers. After rebranding to Nextag in 2000, it quickly gained popularity during the dot-com boom era. In 2007, it was acquired by Wize Commerce and subsequently rebranded as NexTag Inc. In 2014, the company transformed into Connexity, focusing on advertising and marketing technology solutions, evolving its services to meet the changing needs of online marketing and e-commerce. Please note that further developments may have occurred since my last knowledge update in January 2022.
